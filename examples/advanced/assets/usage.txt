$ hello [options] [*command]

Usage:
  hello say <name> [-h]
  hello now [-h]
  hello help [<command>]
  hello [version | --version | -v ]

Commands:
  now  Show actual date and time
  say  Say hello to user

Options:
  --help, -h  Show this help.
  --version   Show version.

Examples:
  hello say David
